About Me & Research Vision

I am a Master’s student in Mathematical Sciences (Data Science Major) at AIMS, Cameroon, with a Bachelor’s in Science Education. My academic training combines strong foundations in mathematics and computation with a growing focus on data-driven research in education and social systems.

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, statistical modeling, educational data science, and social impact. I am dedicated to understanding learning outcomes, structural inequalities, and policy effectiveness.

Long-Term Research Vision

  • Develop interpretable ML models for marginalized regions.
  • Apply causal inference to inform evidence-based policy.
  • Bridge theory and practice for scalable innovations.
  • Advance equity in African education.
